Common Fault Codes

Diagnostic codes provide specific info on turbo actuator failures. Early diagnosis reduces repair costs & prevents damage.

P0299

Turbocharger "A" Underboost

Actual boost pressure lower than commanded.

Suggests insufficient air intake or restricted turbo flow.

P0234

Turbocharger "A" Overboost

Boost pressure exceeds specified limits.

Excessive pressure may cause turbo damage, engine damage, and other component issues.

P2563

Boost Control Position Sensor

Issue with turbocharger boost control position sensor.

Actuator's position reporting is inaccurate.

Early diagnosis & repair using these codes significantly reduces costs & prevents extensive turbo/engine damage.
