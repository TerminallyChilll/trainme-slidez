Installation Preparation

Meticulous preparation of the turbo housing surface and new unit is crucial for proper function and longevity.

Clean Housing Surface

Clean turbo housing surface thoroughly with a wire brush to remove buildup.

Remove Old Gasket Material

Scrape off all old gasket material for effective new gasket seal.

Inspect for Damage

Inspect turbo housing and new actuator for damage; ensure flat surfaces.

Verify New Unit

Visually inspect new VGT actuator for defects and OEM spec match.

Apply Anti-Seize

Apply anti-seize to mounting bolt threads to prevent corrosion.

Position New Gasket

Place new gasket onto turbo housing, aligning with bolt holes.

Align Actuator

Position new actuator onto turbo housing, aligning mounting holes.

Check Vane Engagement

Ensure actuator arm engages VGT vanes during positioning.
