Final Installation Steps

1

Position Actuator

Position new actuator onto turbo housing, aligning with mounting holes & VGT.

2

Start Bolts by Hand

Thread all mounting bolts by hand to prevent cross-threading & ensure seating.

3

Tighten in Star Pattern

Tighten bolts in star pattern with 8mm socket for even pressure.

4

Apply Torque (15-25 Nm)

Use calibrated torque wrench to tighten bolts to 15-25 Nm.

5

Final Check

After torquing, final check all bolts for secure fastening & correct spec.

Precision Torque
