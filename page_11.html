Access & Removal Procedure

Initial Access

1

Remove Engine Cover

Safely remove engine cover for turbocharger access.

2

Identify Actuator

Locate turbo actuator on turbo housing.

3

Note Connector Position

Note/photograph electrical connector position & orientation.

4

Press Connector Tab

Gently press electrical connector release tab.

5

Pull Straight Out

Pull connector straight out; avoid twisting/yanking.

Removing the Actuator

6

Inspect for Corrosion

Inspect actuator & harness connectors for corrosion/damage.

7

Use 8mm Socket

Use 8mm socket for mounting bolts.

8

Remove 3 Mounting Bolts

Remove 3 mounting bolts securing actuator.

9

Keep Bolts Organized

Organize removed bolts to prevent loss.

Caution: Prevent injury from sharp edges & hot components.
