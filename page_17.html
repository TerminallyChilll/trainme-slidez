Common Mistakes to Avoid

Over-tightening Bolts

Strips threads or cracks housing, requiring costly repairs.

Follow torque specifications exactly (15-25 Nm)

Forcing Electrical Connections

Bends pins or damages connectors, causing poor contact.

Clean connections before disassembly

Skipping Surface Cleaning

Compromises gasket seal, leading to leaks & reduced performance.

Clean turbo housing thoroughly with wire brush

Rushing the Installation

Increases errors or missed steps, resulting in poor performance.

Take your time to ensure precision

Inadequate Testing

Leads to unresolved issues or premature actuator failure.

Complete both static tests and road test verification

Pro Tip

Document entire process with photos for future reference.
