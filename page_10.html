Pre-Work Checklist

Complete these essential steps before starting replacement for a smooth and successful installation.

Photograph Connections

Take clear photos of all connections before disassembly for accurate reassembly.

Clean Work Area

Ensure a clean, clutter-free work area to prevent hazards and lost tools.

Organize Tools

Organize all required tools and parts for easy access before starting.

Review Service Manual

Consult service manual for specific torque specs and procedures.

Pro Tip: Review the service manual's electrical diagram for the turbo actuator system.
